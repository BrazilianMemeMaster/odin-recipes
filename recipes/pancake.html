Fluffy Vegan Pancakes

Description

It's litteraly just mixing all ingredients and put in a pan.

Ingedients

  • Flour
  • Soy Milk
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Sugar
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Put all the dry ingredients in a bowl and mix
  2. Put all the wheat ingredients and mix
  3. Put the mixture in a frying pan in medium heat
  4. Turn to the other side when well toasted
  5. Serve with some agave syrup or maple syrup
